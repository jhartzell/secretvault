# Code of Conduct

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in project issues, pull requests, discussions, events, and project-linked social channels.

## Who it applies to

All maintainers, contributors, and participants.

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Focus on technical substance, not personal attacks.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or abusive language.
- Doxxing or sharing private information.
- Sustained disruption, trolling, or intimidation.

## Enforcement and Reporting

Report incidents privately to: conduct@example.com

If the primary contact is involved, report to: alt-contact@example.com

## Consequences

1. Warning
2. Temporary suspension
3. Permanent ban
